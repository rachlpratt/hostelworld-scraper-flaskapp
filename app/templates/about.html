About Hostel Price Explorer


This site was created for travelers to explore the prices of hostels around the world.


Adventurous travelers can generate a random destination and see hostel prices in that location.


For travelers who already have a particular destination in mind, the Destinations page allows users to select from a list of destinations.



This site obtains its price data from the Hostelworld website.
